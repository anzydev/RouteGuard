🔴 The Problem

Supply chain disruptions cost $4.2T annually

Weather events, traffic congestion, and roadblocks cause cascading failures across global supply networks. Logistics teams lack real-time visibility and rely on manual, reactive decision-making — leading to delays, spoiled cargo, and massive financial losses.

73% of companies faced supply disruptions in 2024
45min avg delay per disruption event
₹2.5L avg cost per delayed shipment
6hrs manual response time

💡 Our Solution

An AI Decision Engine
for supply chain resilience

🗺️
Real-Time Map Simulation
Interactive dark-themed Leaflet map with live truck markers, animated route polylines, and click-to-place disruption zones covering Indian logistics corridors.
🧠
AI-Powered Decisions
Automated recommendations — reroute via Dijkstra pathfinding, delay shipments, or split cargo — with confidence scores and cost/time impact analysis.
⚡
Cascade Risk Detection
Graph-based risk propagation that simulates how one disruption cascades through interconnected supply chain nodes with iterative decay modeling.

✨ Key Features

Everything a logistics team needs

🗺️ Interactive Dark Map
CartoDB dark tiles with animated truck markers, click-to-place disruption zones, and fly-to-selected navigation
📊 Live Metrics Dashboard
Animated counter bar showing on-time, at-risk, delayed, cost impact, delay estimates, and risk sparkline — all in real time
🔊 Immersive Sound Effects
Web Audio API synthesized tones for clicks, alerts, warnings, and critical events — no external audio files needed
⚗️ What-If Sandbox Mode
Toggle sandbox mode to experiment with disruption scenarios without affecting the live simulation state
🎛️ Disruption Controls
Global weather slider, traffic level buttons, and point-and-click disruption placement with toggle/remove controls
🔔 Toast Notifications
Glass-morphism slide-in toasts with severity colors, auto-dismiss timer bars, and click-to-dismiss interaction
🧠 AI Decision Cards
Reroute/Delay/Split recommendations with confidence %, cost/delay impact metrics, and one-click Apply action
🌐 Cinematic Splash Screen
Animated wireframe globe with city dots, mesh gradients, floating particles, and shimmer loading bar
